?CANSys - BusOff Failure on CAN 2.

The Controller Area Network (CAN) bus status is monitored cyclically. Whenever there is a short circuit between CAN-H and CAN-L or a heavy bus disturbance that leads to BusOff, the fault is registered. 

This indicates a short circuit between CAN + and CAN-; a short circuit between CAN + and Ubatt; or a heavy bus disturbance.?
