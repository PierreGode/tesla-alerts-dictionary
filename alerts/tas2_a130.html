TAS2_a130_openCircuitFR
This alert checks for open circuit faults every 10ms. Voltage and current are measured by the Tesla Air Suspension Controller (TASC) when the front right (FR) valve is commanded with 12V.

Set: The FR valve is commanded and both of the following are true:
  • voltage is sensed between 11.4 - 12.6V
  • current is sensed below 0.02A
Clear: Single drive-rail cycle
