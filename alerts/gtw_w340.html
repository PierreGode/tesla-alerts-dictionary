GTW_w340_12VTooMuchDischarge
This alert will appear on when one of the following:
  • The 12V electronics draw too much sleep current from the 12v battery.
  • The Gateway is prevented from sleeping even though it has entered the discharge 12V state.

The thresholds for the above conditions are as follows:
  • More than 100mA of sleep current.
  • Unable to sleep for more than 1 hour.
