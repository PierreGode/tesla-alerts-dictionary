PMF_a011_phaseCurrentIrrational
Pedal Monitor Warning: Phase Current Irrational.
The safety monitor sets this condition when phase current sensor measurements are irrational or out of bounds.
This alert will clear when phase current sensor measurements are considered rational.
Pedal Monitor Warning: PM CAN node is missing.
The safety monitor sets this condition when CAN communication from PM is considered irrational or missing.
PM is considered not communicating on CAN for remainder of drive cycle.
