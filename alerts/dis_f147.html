DIS_f147_highTorqueWearLimit
This alert was formerly named DIS_f147_overtorqueUnavailable in firmware versions prior to 2017.6 (17.6.15). If DIS_f147_overtorqueUnavailable appears on the vehicle or in the logs, update the vehicle firmware to the current version.

The algorithm behind this alert has evolved over time and may continue to evolve. While DIS_f147_overtorqueUnavailable actively limited torque levels to prevent Drive Unit malfunction, the new alert, DIS_f147_highTorqueWearLimit, reminds customers to take it easy but does not actively limit torque levels.
