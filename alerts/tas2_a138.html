TAS2_a138_shortCircuitFR
This alert checks for short circuit faults every 10ms. Voltage and current are measured by the Tesla Air Suspension Controller (TASC) when the front right (FR) valve is commanded with 12V.

Set: The FR valve is commanded and both of the following are true:
  • voltage is sensed below 2.4V
  • current is sensed below 0.02A or above 1.3A
Clear: Single drive-rail cycle
