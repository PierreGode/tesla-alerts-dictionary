ESP_d125_rcmSerialNumMismatch
This alert indicates there is a serial number mismatch between the Electrically Erasable Programmable Read-Only Memory (EEPROM) and the Controller Area Network (CAN). The serial number of the Amplified Basic Pressure (ABP) is saved in the EEPROM of the Electronic Stability Program (ESP) electronic control unit (ECU) does not conform with serial number received from airbag ECU.
