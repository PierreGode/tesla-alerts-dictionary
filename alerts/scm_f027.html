SCM_f027_externalIsolationLow
Alert may be shown on the supercharger while a car is charging. This alert may indicate an insulation breakdown or other loss of isolation resistance in one of the following:
  • The DC cabling between the supercharger and the car.
  • The DC cabling between the charge port and high voltage junction box (HVJB),
  •  If while charging, anywhere inside the vehicle's high voltage DC system.
