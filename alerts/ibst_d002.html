IBST_d002_tle5012SsccrcFail
A Cyclic Redundancy Check (CRC) failure in data transmission by SSC / Serial Peripheral Interface (SPI) to the TLE5012 occurred. A CRC is sent from the TLE5012 to the electronic control unit (ECU) for all the data sent from or received by the ECU. The ECU calculates the CRC and compares what is received.? If this alert is present, the calculated and received CRC does not match.?
