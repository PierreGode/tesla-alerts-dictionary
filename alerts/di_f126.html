DI_f126_limpMode
The motor controller shall assert this condition whenever any of the following alerts are present:
  • Phase Current Imbalance
  • Bus Voltage Sensor Irrational
  • No Functional Stator Temperature Sensors
  • No Functional Isolated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) Heatsink Temperature Sensors
  • No Fluid in IGBT Heatsink
  • 12V Supply Undervoltage
  • Battery Management System (BMS) is missing (MIA)
  • Low Fluid Flow

This condition will also occur whenever any of the temperatures listed below exceed their Limp thresholds for the specified time:
  • Outlet fluid (2 seconds)
  • Internal ambient (2 seconds)
