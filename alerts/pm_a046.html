PM_a046_inconsVehicleHoldState
Pedal Monitor Warning: Inconsistent Vehicle Hold state.
The safety monitor sets this condition when sensor or other ECU inputs into vehicle hold are deemed implausible.
The safety monitor will disable vehicle hold for the remainder of the drive cycle.
Pedal Monitor Warning: DI Heartbeat is missing.
The safety monitor sets this condition when DI Heartbeat communication is too fast or too slow.
This alert will clear when DI Heartbeat communication are considered rational.
The safety monitor will initiate a unit limp condition for the remainder of the drive cycle.
Pedal Monitor Warning: Excessive amount of stability control actuation.
