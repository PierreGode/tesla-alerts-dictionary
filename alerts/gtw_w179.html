GTW_w179_accRailMismatch

The Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) rail voltage does not match what the vehicle is expecting. There are three scenarios that can cause this alert to appear:

  1. The ACC rail was requested "On" by the Gateway, but it is stuck in the "Off" state. This is possibly due to a relay that will not unlatch.
  2. The ACC rail was requested "On" when it is already in the "On" state. This is most likely a bad sense lead in the Gateway.
  3. The ACC rail was requested "Off" and it is stuck in the "On" state. This is possibly due to a relay that will not unlatch?.
