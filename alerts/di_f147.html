DI_f147_highTorqueWearLimit
This alert was formerly named DI_f147_overtorqueUnavailable prior to FW version 2017.6 (17.6.15)?.  If DI_f147_overtorqueUnavailable is present on the vehicle or in the logs, update the vehicle firmware to the current version.

The algorithm behind this alert has evolved over time and may continue to evolve. While the previous alert, DI_f147_overtorqueUnavailable, actively limited torque levels to prevent Drive Unit malfunctions, the new alert, DI_f147_highTorqueWearLimit, reminds customers to take it easy but does not actively limit torque levels.?
