TAS2_a129_openCircuitFL
This alert checks for open circuit faults every 10ms. Voltage and current are measured by Tesla Air Suspension Controller (TASC) when the front left (FL) valve is commanded with 12V.

Set: The FL valve is commanded and both of the following are true:
  • voltage is sensed between 11.4 - 12.6V
  • current is sensed below 0.02A
Clear: Single drive-rail cycle
