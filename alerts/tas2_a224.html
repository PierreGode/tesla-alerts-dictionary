TAS2_a224_openAirline
The alert checks for blocked airline faults every 100ms.

Set: The pressure derivative is less than 0.1 Bar/second for 10 seconds and Tesla Air Suspension (TAS) is in one of the following states:
  • TAS_state = Fill_reservoir_from_Compressor
  • TAS_state = Fill_spring_reservoir_from_Compressor
  • TAS_state = Fill_spring_from_Compressor
  • TAS_state = Fill_spring_from_Boost
Clear: Single drive-rail cycle

When the system is functioning normally, the gallery pressure should rise faster than this while filling a spring or the reservoir.
