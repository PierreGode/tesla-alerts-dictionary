BMS_f133_SW_Service_Tool

This alert is triggered when a SW tool (e.g. Toolbox) connected to the vehicle requests an action from the Battery Management System (BMS) that is either improperly requested or is not permitted in the current BMS state.


This alert clears immediately after it is set, and it is only used to inform the customer of an BMS action that is not permitted or is improperly requested.
