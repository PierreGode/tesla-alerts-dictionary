BMS_f021_SW_Dr_Limits_Regulation
To protect the pack and bricks from overvoltage, undervoltage, and overcurrent conditions, this alert is caused by the following:
  • Regen current is too high.
  • Discharge current is too high.
  • Pack voltage is too high.
  • Pack voltage is too low.
  • Brick voltage is too high.
  • Brick voltage is too low.
