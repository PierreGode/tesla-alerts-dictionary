TAS2_a133_openCircuitReservoir
This alert checks for open circuit faults every 10ms. Voltage and current are measured by the Tesla Air Suspension Controller (TASC) when the reservoir valve is commanded with 12V.

Set: The reservoir valve is commanded and both of the following are true:
  • voltage is sensed between 11.4 - 12.6V
  • current is sensed below 0.02A
Clear: Single drive-rail cycle
