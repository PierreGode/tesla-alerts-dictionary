CP_a112_inletHeaterIrrationalTemperature
The charge port read an irrational temperature on the inlet heater temperature pin after the heater has been enabled.
The inlet heater temperature was lower than -30C after 30 seconds of being active.
Alert clears automatically 30 seconds after being set.
The inlet heater's functionality may be compromised. Frozen ice in the charge port may be difficult to remove.
Retries for loading the PLC modem application expired
The PLC modem application fails to load after multiple consecutive attempts
The modem application loads successfully, or the vehicle enters drive state
DC charging will not start with CCS supercharger
