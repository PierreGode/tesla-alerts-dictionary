TPMS_531500TPMS_005315-WE-SensorMutefailureRR
This DTC is for the Continental TPMS 

This DTC lights the TPMS Tell-Tale on the cluster: True/False: ON

This DTC indicates that the receiver did not receive any message from the right rear sensor for 9 minutes during driving. The most likely cause is RF transmitter fault in wheel electronic (WE) sensor.
