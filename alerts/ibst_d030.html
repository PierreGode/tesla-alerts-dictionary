The iBooster motor does not move or moves in the wrong direction. Two monitoring paths are implemented:
  • rotation direction detection
  • oscillation detection

The failure will be set if either an oscillation of the motor was detected or the motor does not move or moves in the wrong direction. The presence of this indicates rotor angle failures, current measurement failures, or mechanical damage of iBooster motor or gear.?
