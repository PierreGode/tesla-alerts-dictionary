TAS2_a144_shortCircuitCompressor
Vehicles without efuse/Vehicle Controller Front (VCFRONT): Tesla Air Suspension (TAS) has determined a short circuit condition on the compressor relay drive circuit. Alert will be set if output is shorted to ground or shorted to 12V.

Vehicles with efuse/VCFRONT: efuse/VCFRONT has determined a short circuit condition on the compressor power drive circuit.
