BMS_a109_HW_BMB_Mute_Status_Mismatch
A BMB device has a MUTE status that does not align with the MUTE/UNMUTE command sent by the BMS
MUTE is disabled for any device while balancing, or otherwise enabled for any device
MUTE is enabled for all devices while balancing, or otherwise disabled for all devices
Bleeding bricks that do not MUTE during balancing can impact SOC estimation
