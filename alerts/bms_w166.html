BMS_w166_SW_AhToBeCharged_Imbal
This alert is for engineering visibility. It does not mean there is an issue with the pack, and the pack should not be replaced due to the presence of this alert.

The alert is reported when the delta between the brick that needs the least amp hours (Ah) compared to the one that needs the most Ah is greater than 15Ah.
  • Alert Set Condition: (maxAhToBeCharged - minAhToBeCharged) > 15Ah  ??
