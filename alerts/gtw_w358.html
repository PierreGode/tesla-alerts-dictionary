GTW_w358_unlockRequiredForStart
This alert may be present on vehicles running firmware version 17.28.103 or later. This alert appears when the following conditions are present:
  • Passive Entry is disabled.
  • The drive rail is OFF, and the vehicle is not started.
  • No key fob unlock command was seen by the vehicle in the last 5 minutes

If the vehicle is remotely unlocked, this alert will also be displayed when the vehicle is not started within 5 minutes.
