DI_w127_gracefulPowerOff
The Drive Inverter has detected that the vehicle has begun a Graceful Power Off.

The motor controller will assert this condition whenever any of the following alerts or controller area network (CAN) signals are present:
  • No Battery Power (any amount of time)
  • GTW_notEnough12VforDrive (any amount of time)

This alert will also trigger whenever any of the temperatures below exceed their Graceful Power Off thresholds for the specified time:
  • Outlet fluid (2 seconds)
  • Internal ambient (2 seconds)
  • Any of the Isolated Gate Bipolar Transistor (IGBT) heatsinks (3 seconds)
  • Stator (2 seconds)
  • Fluid delta T too positive (2 seconds)
