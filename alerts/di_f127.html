DI_f127_gracefulPowerOff

The motor controller asserts this condition whenever any of the following alerts or controller area network (CAN) signals are present:

  • No Battery Power (any amount of time).
  • GTW_notEnough12VforDrive (any amount of time).
  • Whenever any of the temperatures below exceed their Graceful Power Off thresholds for specified time:
    • Outlet fluid (2 seconds)
    • Internal ambient (2 seconds)
    • Any of the IGBT heatsinks (3 seconds)
    • Stator (2 seconds)
    • Fluid delta T too positive (2 seconds)
