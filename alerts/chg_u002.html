CHG_u002_LineImpedanceTooHigh
The line impedance is too high, or the line voltage is dropping significantly from another source drawing current on the same circuit.

If this alert is present, the voltage, which is monitored continuously during charging, has dropped by 14% or more for a period longer than 1 second from the initial voltage sample value that was taken at the beginning of charge before current started flowing and the vehicle will not be able to charge.
