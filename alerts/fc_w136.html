FC_w136_CA_evseTxBufOvf
A Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) TX buffer overflow has occurred, and the messages are not being delivered on the EVSE controller area network (CAN).?

This alert was formerly named FC_w136_CA_evseTxBufOvf_PT in firmware versions prior to 2017.12. If FC_w136_CA_evseTxBufOvf_PT appears on the vehicle or in the logs, update the vehicle firmware to the current version.
