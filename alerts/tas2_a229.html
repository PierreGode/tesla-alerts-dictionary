TAS2_a229_PWMfastDetectRR
Alert is set to indicate that the rear right (RR) height sensor temporarily faulted when 100%, 0%, or a large delta in duty cycle was read from the RR pulse width modulation (PWM) height-sensor.

Duty cycle of 100% indicates an open circuit. Duty cycle of 0% indicates a short to ground. The alert will clear when one second of expected values is received from the RR height-sensor.
