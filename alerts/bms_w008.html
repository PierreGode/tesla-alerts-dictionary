BMS_w008_HW_HVIL
The Battery Management System (BMS) is reporting that there is a hardware issue with the high voltage interlocking loop (HVIL).

If this alert is present, the vehicle may have been in Drive and the contactors did not open. Once the vehicle exits Drive, the alert will become a fault while in support mode, and the vehicle may not be able to support 12V, charge, or re-enter Drive.
