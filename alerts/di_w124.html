DI_w124_cruiseFault
The Drive Unit has detected an issue with the cruise feature.

If this alert is present, there may have been an invalid brake switch signal when the cruise feature is Enabled or is in Standby. There may be an issue with the Controller Area Network (CAN) bus communication components related to the cruise feature, and there may also be alerts related to the motor encoder, the pedal monitor, and / or the CAN bus.
