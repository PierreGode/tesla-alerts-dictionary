Pedal Monitor Warning: Stability Control Inhibited.
The safety monitor has detected that stability control is interfering adversely with driving ability. This shall be accompanied by other stability control alerts to aid diagnosis.
The safety monitor has disabled stability control. Customer facing alert that stability control is disabled. Regenerative braking and certain AP features are now disabled.

This alert will clear when accelerator pedal sensor measurements are considered rational.
