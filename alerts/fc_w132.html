FC_w132_CA_bmsMia
This fast charge alert is triggered when the CHAdeMO adapter has not received a Battery Management System (BMS) controller area network (CAN) message for 2.5 seconds while actively charging (or during the startup sequence).?

This alert was formerly named FC_w132_CA_bmsMia_PT and FC_w132_CA_bmsMia_EVSE in firmware versions prior to 2020.04. If FC_w132_CA_bmsMia_PT or FC_w132_CA_bmsMia_EVSE appear on the vehicle or in the logs, update the vehicle firmware to the current version.
