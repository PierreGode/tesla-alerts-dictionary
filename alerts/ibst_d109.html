IBST_d109_micInitSpiTransfErr
The data transfer to the application-specific integrated circuit (ASIC) (System / Valve / RFP ASIC) through the Serial peripheral interface (SPI) lines has failed during initialization. This could indicate a defect in the ASIC (system ASIC/Valve ASIC/RFP ASIC), SPI line interruptions, or that the wrong data is being sent to the ASIC.
