DI_f035_phaseCurrentBalance
A motor current imbalance is detected.

This alert will falsely trip due to:
  • The alert DI_w035_phaseCurrentBalance triggered when the vehicle was going into Standby mode.
  • The alert is accompanied by the alert DI_w112_noPhaseCurrent.

Otherwise, this alert may be accompanied by other phase alerts, which point to a general fault with the phase current regulation.
