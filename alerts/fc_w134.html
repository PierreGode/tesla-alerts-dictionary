FC_w134_CA_vehTxBufOvf
A vehicle RX buffer overflow has occurred, and the messages are not being delivered on the vehicle controller area network (CAN). This issue can be common on some models of Nissan CHAdeMO stations.

This alert was formerly named FC_w134_CA_vehTxBufOvf_PT in firmware versions prior to 2017.12. If FC_w134_CA_vehTxBufOvf_PT appears on the vehicle or in the logs, update the vehicle firmware to the current version.
