TPMS_531200TPMS_005312-WE-SensorMutefailureFL
This DTC is for the Continental TPMS. This DTC lights the TPMS Tell-Tale on the cluster: True/False: ON


This DTC indicates that the receiver did not receive any message from the Front Left sensor for 9 minutes during driving. The most likely cause is RF transmitter fault in wheel electronic (WE) sensor.
