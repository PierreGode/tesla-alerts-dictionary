VCFRONT_a494_12VBatteryTempBlockingOTA
The 12V Battery is not within an acceptable temperature range to support an OTA update.
The temperature of the 12V Battery drops below -20 degrees Celcius for more than 5 consecutive seconds.
The temperature of the 12V Battery rises above -20 degrees Celcius for more than 60 consecutive seconds.
The vehicle will block OTA updates to prevent power loss during OTA sequence.
