DI_u002_shiftDenied
The drive inverter has prevented the shifting between gear selections based on one of the following criteria not being met:
  • If the vehicle is shifting from Drive to Reverse or Reverse to Drive, the vehicle's speed must be lower than 5mph.
  • If the vehicle is shifting from Drive or Reverse or Neutral to Park, the vehicle's speed must be lower than 1.4mph in any direction.
  • If the vehicle is shifting from Park or Neutral to Drive or Reverse, the vehicle has been in P or N for more than 1 second, and the vehicle's speed is less than 5mph, the accelerator must be engaged at less than 5% torque.
