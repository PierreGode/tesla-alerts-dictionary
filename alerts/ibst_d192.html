IBST_d192_dlCorCSumVin
IBST_d192_dlCorCSumVin is also known as IBST_522081.

Invalid Data Received From Gateway (VIN-Message): Data Length Code, Checksum or Parity invalid. ComScl: VIP_405HS - DataLengthCode error. 

This could indicate a physical controller area network (CAN) communication problem.
