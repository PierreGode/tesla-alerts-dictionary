BMS_f030_SW_Chg_Port_Door_Open
The battery management system (BMS) has detected that the charge port door is open or the fast charge (FC) contactors are welded.

If this alert is present, the high voltage (HV) contactors will be prevented from closing. This means the HV system will not be exposed to the current if the FC contactors are welded or the charge port door is open.
