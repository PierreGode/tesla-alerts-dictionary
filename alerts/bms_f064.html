BMS_f064_SW_BrickV_Imbalance
The Battery Management System (BMS) has detected that there is a brick voltage imbalance in the high voltage (HV) battery pack.

If this alert is present, maxBrickV - minBrickV may have grown faster than 25 mV while not driving or maxBrickV - minBrickV may have been > 0.5 V for more than 3 hours while the contactors were open.

This alert was formerly named BMS_w064_SW_SOC_Imbalance in firmware versions prior to 17.20.26. If BMS_w064_SW_SOC_Imbalance appears on the vehicle or in the logs, update the vehicle firmware to the current version.
