EFUSE_w159_revBattFaulted
This alert is intended to show that the 12V battery was connected in reverse-polarity (negative post to positive harness and vice-versa) but can trigger during normal operation, which is not expected and is currently being investigated.

For now, this alert can be ignored.

This alert triggered if the reverse battery E-fuse is tripped. It is sometimes triggered along with EFUSE_w148_cabinOverCurrent and EFUSE_w147_frunkOverCurrent as well.
