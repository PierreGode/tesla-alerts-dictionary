IBST_a117_supplyOvervoltageLvl2
The UBB voltage is in overvoltage level2 failure range. System shall be in 'ReducedPerformance' state.
The control over MRG via EN_HY or MRAUC or both does not work. ECU internal fault, check wiring to the unit and/or re-flash. If problem continures after several power cycles, reolace unit.
