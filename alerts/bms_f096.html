BMS_f096_SW_Fast_Charger_MIA
The Battery Management System (BMS) has detected that the fast charger is missing (MIA) due to an issue with the single wire Controller Area Network (CAN) communication between the fast charge hardware, the master charger, or the BMS.

If this alert is present, it may also be set as a secondary alert in cases where the fast charge sequence has been interrupted by another issue.
