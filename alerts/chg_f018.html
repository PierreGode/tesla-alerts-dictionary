CHG_f018_hwLineOC
The charging process was interrupted by the complex programmable logic device (CPLD) due to a very sudden increase of line current above the limit threshold.

The alert may be falsely set due to extreme AC line voltage disturbances from other equipment.

The Gen 1 charger spec defines the alert as line peak over-current trip level shall be <= 85A with a response time < 1us.

The Gen 2 charger spec defines the alert as greater than 33A (tolerance of +/- 1.7A) per phase board.
