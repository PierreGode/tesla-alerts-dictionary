The rear right rebound damping valve circuit is open, shorted, or returns an unexpected resistance. The damper valve is located on the damper body in the wheel well. Compression is the top valve and rebound is the bottom valve. The rear damper valves are connected directly to the subframe harness. The damper valves are driven by the Tesla Air Suspension ECU.

Set: An open circuit, short circuit, or unexpected resistance value is detected on the valve after startup, or if the damper current deviates from the commanded current over time (indicating an unexpected circuit behavior).
Clear: Drive rail cycle or ECU power cycle.
