TPMS_531300TPMS_005313-WE-SensorMutefailureFR
This DTC is for the Continental TPMS

This DTC lights the TPMS Tell-Tale on the cluster: True/False: ON

This DTC indicates that the receiver did not receive any message from the Front Left sensor for 9 minutes during driving. The most likely cause is RF transmitter fault in WE sensor.
